Publications & Work

IEEE CONFERENCE PAPER  ·  2ND AUTHOR

Performance and Efficiency Analysis of Modern Filesystems on Consumer-Grade NVMe SSDs Under Real-World Mixed Workloads

Conference: IEEE (Published)  ·  Role: 2nd Author  ·  Topic: Modern Filesystem Performance

A comprehensive benchmarking study evaluating the throughput, latency, and I/O efficiency of modern filesystems [NTFS, BTRFS, ZFS, XFS & EXT4] on Consumer-Grade NVMe SSDs. This research provides data-driven insights into filesystem behavior and resource overhead under real-world mixed workloads.
